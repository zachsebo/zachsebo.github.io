Zachary Sebo
Postdoctoral Fellow in the Chandel Lab at Northwestern University

I am a postdoctoral fellow at Northwestern University in the laboratory of Navdeep Chandel. I received my PhD in Molecular, Cellular and Developmental Biology from Yale University in 2020.

Currently, my colleagues and I are working to identify novel metabolic dependencies of cancer and to better understand the role of mitochondria in the regulation of whole-body glucose metabolism. My previous research experience involved CRISPR-based genetic tool development and the application of mouse models to study metabolic disease.
